How Snowball Sampling Works

Step 0: Initialize

Start with a small set of seed keywords related to your research topic. These should be obvious, well-known terms in your domain.

Step 1: First Iteration

Search for social media posts containing your seed keywords. Extract additional keywords that frequently co-occur with your seeds.

Step 2: Expansion

Use the newly discovered keywords as additional search terms. This expands your keyword network to include related terms you might not have initially considered.

Step 3: Iteration

Repeat the process with each new set of keywords. Continue until you reach saturation (few new keywords found) or your desired network size.
